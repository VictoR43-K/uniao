About Us

Our Company

Excellence in cultivation, manufacturing, and export

QualityCertified

Uniao Group Limited is a well-established producer of sisal fiber with main production facilities in Nairobi, Kenya. We manage the full value chain from cultivation and processing to spinning and global export.

With strong management and a solid equity base, we supply UG grade, SSUG grade, polished sisal fiber, and sisal yarn, alongside a broad portfolio of agro commodities for international buyers.
